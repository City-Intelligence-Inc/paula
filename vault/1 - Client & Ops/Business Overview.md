# Mathitude — Business Overview

## Core Business
- **What**: K-12 math enrichment and tutoring
- **Who**: Students pre-K through college; parents as key stakeholders
- **Where**: San Francisco Bay Area (in-person + virtual)
- **Founded**: 2013

## Revenue Streams
1. **Private tutoring** — individual sessions (from ~$90/hr)
2. **Group tutoring** — small group sessions (from ~$50/student)
3. **Published workbooks** — sold via Amazon/bookstores
4. **Math festivals/events** — community engagement, brand building

## Key Metrics
- **Demand > Supply** — perpetual waitlist
- Solo operator handling all instruction
- Over a decade of operation

## Pain Points
1. More demand than capacity → waitlist
2. All instruction depends on Paula personally
3. Books need better advertising/distribution
4. Systems are being built ad-hoc by Sarah
5. No scalable client management platform

## Philosophy
- Augmentation, not automation
- Human connection is the product
- Collaboration over competition
- Mindfulness-informed practice
- Fun-first engagement approach
